# Open Desk — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

Open Desk ("we", "us") operates the Open Desk customer mobile application (the "App"),
a prepaid bulk-SMS reseller service for organizations in Tanzania. This policy explains
what data we collect, why, and your rights. Host this document at a public URL and supply
that URL in Google Play Console and App Store Connect.

## Data We Collect

| Data | Why we collect it | Legal basis |
|------|-------------------|-------------|
| **National Identification Number (NIN/NIDA)** | Identity verification (KYC) — the core trust feature; we verify every account against the NIDA registry | Contract performance + legal/anti-abuse |
| **Phone number** | Account identity, login, and the destination for the Azampay mobile-money payment prompt | Contract performance |
| **Email address** | Account communications and receipts | Contract performance |
| **Payment data** | Processing prepaid SMS bundle purchases via Azampay (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Halopesa, AzamPesa). We store the transaction reference and amount; we do **not** store your mobile-money PIN | Contract performance |
| **Contacts you add** | Delivering the bulk SMS campaigns you create. These belong to you; we process them only to send your messages | Contract performance |
| **Campaign content & delivery metadata** | Sending messages and showing you sent/delivered/failed counts | Contract performance |

## How We Use Data
- Verify your identity (NIDA) before enabling sending.
- Process bundle purchases and maintain your prepaid balance.
- Deliver the SMS campaigns you compose to the recipients you choose.
- Provide delivery reporting and account support.

## Data Sharing
- **Azampay** — to process mobile-money payments.
- **Our upstream SMS provider** — to deliver your messages.
- **NIDA** — to verify your identity at registration.
We do not sell your personal data or your contacts.

## Data Retention
We retain account, payment, and campaign records for as long as your account is active and
as required by Tanzanian law, then delete or anonymize them.

## Your Rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may
close your account. Contact us at the address below.

## Children
The App is intended for organizations and adults; it is not directed at children under 13.
