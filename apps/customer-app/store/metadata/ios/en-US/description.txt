Open Desk lets small organizations in Tanzania send bulk SMS to their members in minutes — verified, trusted, and without any technical knowledge.

• NIDA-verified accounts — every sender is identity-checked with their National ID.
• Pay with mobile money — buy prepaid SMS bundles via M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Halopesa, and AzamPesa.
• Simple contact management — add your members and message them directly.
• Send in minutes — compose, pick recipients, and dispatch a campaign in a few taps.
• Track delivery — see sent, delivered, and failed counts for every campaign.
• Built for Tanzania — full Swahili and English support, TZS pricing, local payments.

Open Desk is a prepaid SMS reseller for churches, schools, SACCOs, cooperatives, and community groups that need a simple, trustworthy way to reach their people.
